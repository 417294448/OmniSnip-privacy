# OmniSnip Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

## Overview

OmniSnip is a browser extension that provides screenshot capture and image editing functionality. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

OmniSnip does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal information or screenshot data to external servers. All data is processed and stored locally on your device.

## Local Storage

- **Screenshots**: All captured screenshots are stored locally in your browser's storage (IndexedDB) or downloaded to your device.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (capture mode, output format, quality settings) are stored locally using Chrome's storage API.
- **Gallery Data**: Image metadata is stored locally on your device.

## Data Usage

The extension uses the captured screenshots only for:
- Displaying the screenshot in the built-in editor
- Saving screenshots to your device
- Managing screenshots in the local gallery

## Third-Party Disclosure

OmniSnip does not share any data with third parties. All processing happens locally on your device.

## Permissions

OmniSnip requests the following permissions:
- `storage` - To save your settings and screenshots locally
- `downloads` - To save screenshots to your device
- `scripting` - To enable screenshot functionality
- `notifications` - To notify you when screenshots are saved
- `<all_urls>` - Required to capture screenshots on any webpage

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the extension.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the extension's GitHub repository.
